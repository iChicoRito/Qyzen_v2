# Objective
## Manage Exemptions Validation Fix & Special Access Duration Investigation

---

## Role

You are a QA/product engineer working on the educator-facing exemption and special access management features. Approach this objective with attention to UX flow, form validation logic, and notification triggers.

---

## Description
This objective covers two related but distinct workstreams on the educator side of the platform. The first involves refining the validation logic in the Manage Exemptions feature so that unchecking previously selected students (without adding any new ones) does not trigger a required "reason" field, since no new exemption is actually being created. The current behavior incorrectly treats this as requiring a reason, which results in unnecessary or duplicate notifications being sent to students. The second workstream involves investigating how the Manage Special Access feature currently works, specifically how the access duration is defined for students who are granted extra time to complete past-due or expired quizzes. The goal is to clarify the exact duration rule currently in place and determine whether a recommendation should be made for improving or standardizing it.

---

## Primary Objective
Fix the Manage Exemptions reason-field validation so it is not required when students are only being unchecked (deselected) and no new exemption is being added, in order to prevent unnecessary student notifications.

---

## Secondary Objectives
- Investigate and document how the Manage Special Access duration currently works (e.g., 24 hours, 1 hour, only valid on the day access is granted).
- Determine whether a recommendation is needed for the special access duration rule, and raise clarifying questions to the requester before finalizing.

---

## Constraints
- The reason field should remain required when new students are being added/selected for an exemption; the fix applies only to the uncheck/deselect scenario.

---

## Context & Dependencies
- Manage Exemptions currently requires a reason when selecting single or multiple students for exemption, and this validation is functioning correctly for that case.
- Manage Special Access currently allows educators to grant students access to take due or expired quizzes, but the exact duration logic is not yet confirmed.

---

## Supporting Tasks

### Manage Exemptions
- Update validation logic so that unchecking students without adding new ones does not require the reason field
- Verify that the reason field still remains required when new students are selected for exemption
- Confirm that this fix prevents duplicate or unnecessary notifications from being sent to students

### Manage Special Access Investigation
- Investigate current implementation to determine how long students retain special access (e.g., 24 hours, 1 hour, only on the day granted)
- Document findings on the current duration behavior
- Prepare clarifying questions for the requester regarding a possible recommendation, and await their input before proposing a duration rule

---

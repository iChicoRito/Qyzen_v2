# Objective

## Enhance Archive Management, Assessment Publishing, and Academic Term Visibility

## Description

Expand the educator and admin functionality by introducing archive management for question banks and scores, improving assessment publishing controls, and adding academic term visibility management. Educators should be able to archive and restore question bank batches and scores through dedicated archive pages while maintaining organized batch-based management. Additionally, assessment creation and editing should support publishing without notifying students. On the administrative side, academic terms should be configurable as active or inactive, with inactive terms automatically hidden across all Educator and Student portals.

## Primary Objective

Implement archive management, assessment publishing controls, and academic term visibility management while preserving a clear, organized user experience across the Educator and Admin portals.

## Secondary Objectives

- Add archive and bulk restore functionality for question bank batches.
- Add archive and restore functionality for assessment scores.
- Organize archived question banks by their upload batches.
- Introduce an **Active (Publish Only – No Notification)** option during assessment creation and editing.
- Allow administrators to activate or deactivate academic terms.
- Hide all data belonging to inactive academic terms from Educator and Student portals.

## Success Criteria

- Educators can archive question bank batches and restore them in bulk.
- A dedicated **Archived Questions** page is available from the sidebar.
- Archived questions remain organized according to their original upload batches.
- Educators can archive and restore scores through a dedicated archive page.
- Assessment creation and editing include an **Active (Publish Only – No Notification)** option that publishes assessments without sending student notifications.
- Administrators can toggle academic terms between **Active** and **Inactive**.
- Only data belonging to active academic terms is visible throughout the Educator and Student portals.

## Context & Dependencies

- The Question Bank feature already supports uploaded question batches.
- The Educator sidebar must be extended to include an **Archived Questions** menu.
- The existing Score module should be extended with archive and restore functionality.
- Assessment creation and editing workflows already exist and must be extended without disrupting current functionality.
- Academic Terms already exist and serve as the organizational basis for portal data visibility.

## Detailed Breakdown

### Question Bank Archive System

Implement an archive mechanism for uploaded question bank batches that allows educators to archive entire batches instead of individual questions. Archived questions should be accessible from a dedicated **Archived Questions** page in the Educator sidebar, remain organized by their original upload batches, and support bulk restoration of complete batches.

### Score Archive System

Extend the existing score management module with archive and restore capabilities. Educators should be able to archive scores, access them through a dedicated archived scores page, and restore archived scores whenever necessary.

### Assessment Publishing Options

Enhance both assessment creation and editing workflows by introducing an **Active (Publish Only – No Notification)** option. This allows educators to publish assessments while intentionally suppressing notifications sent to students.

### Academic Term Visibility

Provide administrators with the ability to activate or deactivate academic terms. When an academic term is marked as inactive, all associated data must be hidden from both Educator and Student portals. Reactivating the term should immediately restore the visibility of its associated data across the system.

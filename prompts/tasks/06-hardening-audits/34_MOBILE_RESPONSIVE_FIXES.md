# Objective
## Mobile Devices Proper Responsive

---

## Description
On the student-facing side of the application, all pages currently exhibit unresponsive behavior. Instead of adapting to the fixed width of the screen, these pages allow horizontal scrolling, which indicates a failure in responsive design. The provided screenshots illustrate examples of this broken and non-responsive layout. The objective is to systematically explore every student-facing page, identify all instances where the layout does not properly respond to mobile screen widths, and correct those issues to eliminate horizontal scrolling and visual breakage.

---

## Primary Objective
Fix all unresponsive pages on the student side of the application to ensure they adapt correctly to mobile screen widths without horizontal scrolling or layout breakage.

---

## Success Criteria
- All student-facing pages render within the viewport width on mobile devices without horizontal scrolling.
- No layout elements appear broken or overflow their containers on mobile screen sizes.

---

## Out of Scope
- Non-student-facing pages or administrative interfaces.
